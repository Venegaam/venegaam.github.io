These are the wines we currently have somewhere in the process between sitting in a box as juice, concentrate, or whatever, and being drank by you. If a wine is on this page it is either available for purchase, or in some cases expected to be delivered to club members only in a future release. Some were made before I kept studious records of all my wine, and some before I could even get a good reading on acidity, so not all wines have complete information.


Thoughout the process I will endeavor to keep this page updated with the progress and my thoughts about the wines so far. We always hold back a few bottles to drink ourselves over time so that we can see how they are aging, so be sure to check back from time to time, even if you have already read about one of the wines you already have. We may be able to update our notes to even include a recommendation on when to drink it.


Have your own thoughts about the wine? Please feel free to let us know what you think by contacting us. You never know, your thoughts or notes could even make it onto this webpage.
